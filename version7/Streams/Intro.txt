What is Stream?
A Stream is like a pipe that gives you values one after another over time.
💡 Think: water drops coming out of a tap — not all at once, but one by one.

Types of Streams
 1. Single Subscription Stream
 2. Broadcast Stream


Single Subscription Stream:
 1. Allows only one listener
 2. Default type of stream
 3. If you try to listen again → ❌ Error

Broadcast Stream:
 1. Allows multiple listeners
